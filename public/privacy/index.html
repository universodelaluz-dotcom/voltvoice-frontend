Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide when registering or using the platform, such as name, email, plan, token usage, and configuration preferences. When you connect your YouTube account, we access your channel information and live chat messages solely to provide the StreamVoicer service.

2. How we use your information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve voice, AI, and configuration features
  • Access YouTube Live Chat messages to power the live reading feature
  • Manage payments, subscriptions, and token balances
  • Send operational, security, and billing notices
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

3. YouTube API Services

StreamVoicer uses YouTube API Services to read live chat messages from your active YouTube Live stream. By using this feature, you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service.

Google's privacy policy is available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

We only access YouTube data necessary to provide the live chat reading feature. We do not store your YouTube chat messages beyond what is needed for real-time processing.

4. Legal basis and retention

We process your data to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and protect legitimate security and operational interests. We retain information as long as necessary for these purposes.

5. Sharing with third parties

We may share data with providers that help us operate the platform (for example authentication, payments, hosting, analytics, or voice/AI services), under reasonable contractual and security safeguards. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and improve your experience. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

7. Data security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No system is infallible, but we work to reduce risks of unauthorized access or data loss.

8. User rights

You may request access, correction, updating, or deletion of your personal data, and withdraw consent where applicable. You can revoke YouTube API access at any time via Google Security Settings. To exercise your rights, contact us via support email.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect service changes or legal requirements. We will publish the current version at this URL.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: soporte@streamvoicer.com
