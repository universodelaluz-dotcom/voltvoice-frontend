OFFICIAL STREAMVOICER FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about tokens, Base Plan, cloned voice packs, safety, and setup for TikTok LIVE.

Go to main page

Top questions

1

How does Stream Voicer work?

Stream Voicer reads your TikTok LIVE chat messages in real time and plays them with voice to keep interaction constant in your stream.

2

What is a token?

1 token equals 1 generated character. Usage applies when you use premium voices or custom voices.

3

Which voices consume tokens?

Premium voices and custom voices consume tokens. The essential voice does not consume tokens.

4

Does Stream Voicer increase engagement and retention?

Yes. Real-time voice reading keeps chat active, increases participation, reduces dead air, and improves retention and overall stream dynamics.

5

What if I run out of tokens?

You can add a cloned voice pack (Lite, Pro, or Max). If you have no tokens, premium and cloned voices stop playing until you recharge.

6

How much does Base Plan cost?

Base Plan costs $9.99 USD/month and includes 2 unlimited essential voices, 2 premium voices, 20,000 monthly characters, and all key features.

7

What are cloned voice packs?

They are add-ons to Base Plan that increase your AI cloned voice capacity: Lite (+1 voice, 50K tokens), Pro (+3 voices, 250K), Max (+6 voices, 500K).

8

Can I cancel Base Plan anytime?

Yes. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your already paid period.

9

Does it support other languages?

Stream Voicer is optimized for Spanish and English. More languages will be added soon.

10

How long does it take to process a message?

Usually 1 to 3 seconds depending on message length, selected voice, and system load.

11

Is connecting my TikTok safe?

Yes. Only the minimum access to read public LIVE activity is used. We do not share your data with third parties.

12

Can I create and edit cloned voices?

Yes. You can manage cloned voices from your panel. Available slots depend on the pack added to your Base Plan.
